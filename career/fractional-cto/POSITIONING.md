# Fractional CTO — Service Positioning

## The Offer
AI-native technical leadership for startups that need a CTO but can't afford (or don't need) a full-time one.

## What Makes This Different From Every Other Fractional CTO
Traditional fractional CTOs: review your code, hire your engineers, manage your sprints.
Tim's model: **orchestrate AI agents to ship your product**, with quality gates and production-grade infrastructure. The team IS the AI. Tim is the CTO directing it.

## Current Market Rate
- $360/hr through BioSTL (healthcare advisory)
- Non-solicitation agreement in place — cannot directly solicit BioSTL clients
- BioSTL unlikely to be cooperative on referrals
- **Implication:** Build fractional CTO pipeline independently. Healthcare contacts who find Tim organically are fine. Don't approach BioSTL clients directly.

## Target Clients
1. **Non-technical solo founders** with a product idea and some traction
   - Need: someone to build or rebuild their MVP properly
   - Pain: burned by offshore devs, no-code limitations, AI coding chaos
   - Budget: $5-15k/month

2. **Early-stage startups (pre-Series A)** with 1-2 junior devs
   - Need: technical leadership, architecture, quality standards
   - Pain: shipping fast but accumulating tech debt, no one senior reviewing
   - Budget: $10-20k/month

3. **Non-profits / NGOs** with mission-critical but underfunded tech
   - Need: someone who can do more with less
   - Pain: can't compete with startup salaries for talent
   - Budget: $3-8k/month (but deeply loyal, great references)

## Service Tiers (DRAFT)

Anchored against $360/hr current rate. Fractional CTO packages should be at or above that — you're offering more value (building, not just advising).

### Advisory ($4,000/month)
- 8 hours/month (~$500/hr effective but packaged as retainer)
- Architecture review, tech stack decisions, vendor evaluation
- Async access via Slack/email
- Monthly strategy call

### Builder ($12,000/month)
- 20 hours/month
- Active development via AI orchestration
- Sprint planning, feature shipping, quality enforcement
- Weekly sync call + async access

### Embedded ($20,000/month)
- 35+ hours/month
- Act as full CTO — roadmap, hiring guidance, investor prep, tech due diligence
- AI-driven development + strategic leadership

## Lifestyle Fit
Tim's ideal: working a few hours a day from cafes in Porto or beaches in Akumal. This means:
- **Advisory tier** is the sweet spot — high hourly rate, low hours, location-independent
- **Builder tier** works if the client is async-friendly (no "be in our office" expectations)
- **Embedded tier** only for short engagements (3-6 month sprints) or exceptional fit
- Target: 2 Advisory + 1 Builder = $20k/month at ~36 hrs/month. Very doable from anywhere.
