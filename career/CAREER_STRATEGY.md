# Career Strategy — Portfolio Model

## The Vision
Working from cafes in Porto and beaches in Akumal. A few hours a day on things that matter. No 9-to-5. No soul-crushing corporate. Income to travel as much as you want in retirement.

## Revenue Streams

| Stream | Status | Monthly Target | Priority |
|--------|--------|---------------|----------|
| Healthcare advisory (BioSTL) | Active | ~$360/hr (variable) | Maintain — non-solicitation limits growth |
| Fractional CTO (independent) | Not started | $12-20k | **Build this — best lifestyle fit** |
| STP product revenue | Pre-revenue | $1-5k (12mo goal) | Keep building — proof point |
| Podcasts / speaking | Not started | $2-5k | Start with podcasts (free), conferences later |
| Content (courses, workshops) | Not started | $0 initially | Long game |

**Target steady state:** 2 Advisory clients + 1 Builder client = ~$20k/month at ~36 hrs/month. Location-independent. Beach-compatible.

## Constraints
- Non-solicitation with BioSTL — cannot approach their clients directly
- BioSTL unlikely to help with referrals
- Conferences are expensive — prioritize podcasts and virtual events first
- Comms/social media is a weakness — need system and support (this workspace)
